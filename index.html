One Vision. Thousands of Hearts.
Millions of Lives.

A​ster Volunteers is the CSR arm of Aster DM Healthcare, founded on the vision of our ​Founder Chairman Dr. Azad Moopen, who believes that humanity has the power to heal what medicine alone cannot. Since its launch in 2017, Aster Volunteers has united individuals from every walk of life to create lasting impact in the lives of those most in need.

With a footprint across 20 countries and outreach in over 70 regions, the movement has touched more than ​6​.5 million + lives through initiatives in healthcare, education, disaster response, and social empowerment.

At its heart are 85,000+ passionate volunteers, supported by Aster DM Healthcare, Aster DM Foundation (India and International), the Dr. Moopen Family Foundation, and partner institutions, who work together to deliver not just aid, but hope, dignity, and a chance at a better future.

Join us and help build a better world.

Aster Volunteers Across Geographies

We operate across 20 countries, with a presence spanning Africa, Central and West Asia, as well
as South and Southeast Asia. Through our widespread reach, we aim to deliver impactful
healthcare and humanitarian initiatives to underserved communities around the world.
